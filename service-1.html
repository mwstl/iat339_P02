PROBLEM

SOLUTION
  1. GEOGRAPHA

    The building was designed to withstand earthquakes and tsunamis and are flexible to disperse the impacts.

  2. CLIMATE

    Materials were chosen with the capability to expand and contract, while still retaining flexibility.

  3. LIFESTYLE

    Major intersections and traffic areas were taken into account while designing the faces and facades of the building. Certain angles and windowed areas were avoided to prevent angling towards pedestrians throughout the seasons.
